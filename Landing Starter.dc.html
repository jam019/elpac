Learning Objective

Students will be able to talk and write about their school day — letters, numbers, names, people, places, and time — using new vocabulary and complete sentences, with at least 80% accuracy on each lesson’s speaking and writing tasks.

Success Criteria

I am successful when:

  • I can say and write at least 5 new vocabulary words from each lesson.
  • I can answer questions in complete sentences using a sentence frame.
  • I can name people, places, and things at school.
  • I can finish each writing task with correct capital letters and periods.
