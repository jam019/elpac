Learning Objective

Students will be able to speak and write about themselves, their school, their community, and their world using new vocabulary and complete sentences, with at least 80% accuracy on each lesson's writing and speaking tasks.

Success Criteria

I am successful when:

  • I can say and write at least 5 new vocabulary words from each lesson.
  • I can answer questions in complete sentences using a sentence frame.
  • I can read a short text and answer the questions correctly.
  • I can finish each writing task with correct capital letters and periods.
